About Us

Welcome to Wrap And Roll, where egg rolls are reimagined into mouthwatering delights. Located in Bowling Green, KY, our food truck offers a unique twist on traditional egg rolls, featuring flavors like cheeseburger, buffalo chicken, loaded potato, and even dessert options.
At Wrap And Roll, we're passionate about serving up smiles with every bite. Our menu also includes wraps and dessert egg rolls, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy.
Embark on a culinary adventure with us and indulge in egg rolls like never before. Follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest locations and mouthwatering specials. We're excited to have you join us for a taste sensation that's sure to leave you craving more!
